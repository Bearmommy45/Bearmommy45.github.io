What Are Cake Mix Hacks?

There’s something to be said about the convenience of a boxed cake mix. Its dump-and-stir process is appealing to anyone ― amateur bakers, skilled professionals in a rush, or anyone in between. While there’s no shame in getting a little help from Duncan Hines, we don’t want people to know we took a shortcut. Luckily with a few simple tricks, techniques and tweaks, you can fool anyone into thinking you’ve been in the kitchen all day making a cake from scratch.
