How to Hack a Cake Mix

By Nicole Harbour-Ramos

Step 1: Choose Your Cake Mix

The three top brands of store bought cake mix are: Betty Crocker, Pillsbury, and Ducan Hinds.

Pillsbury is the most affordable, followed by Betty Crocker, and then Duncan Hinds.

Decide which flavor of cake you'd like to make.


Step 2: Ingredient List

You will need:

  • 1 box of cake mix
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup oil (sunflower, canola, vegetable, etc.)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or whole milk yogurt
  • 1/2 cup the liquid of your choice (coffee, bourbon, flavored rum, buttermilk, Baileys, etc.)

Suggested combinations: Strawberry Cake Mix and coconut rum, Lemon Cake Mix and strawberry rum, Devil's Food Cake Mix and Baileys, and Vanilla Cake Mix with Tippy Cow Orange Cream Rum.

If you want to get fancy, use flavored whole milk yogurt instead of plain yogurt. Example, White Cake Mix, blueberry yogurt, and blueberry lemonade rum.


Step 3: Mix It Up!

You'll need a large mixing bowl, sifter, hand-held electric mixer,rubber spatula, non-stick cooking spray, and a glass measuring cup with measurements listed on the side.

  • Pre-heat your oven to 350 degrees.
  • Sift the dry cake mix into the mixing bowl.
  • Add in the oil, liquid of your choice, eggs, and yogurt/sourcream.
  • Mix on low speed for 30 seconds. Scrape down the sides of the bowl with the rubber spatula. Mix on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  • Spray the cake pan with non-stick cooking spray and pour the cake mix into the pan. Use the rubber spatula to spread the cake mix evenly into the pan.

Step 4: Bake Your Cake

How long should you bake your cake?

    A cake baked in a 9x13 pan will bake for 30-35 minutes.
    If using two 9-inch round cake pans, bake for 25-30 minutes.
    Bundt cakes bake for 40-45 minutes.
    Cupcakes will bake the quickest at 14-19 minutes.

Cool the cake 10 minutes before removing from pan to cool completely on a wire rack.
